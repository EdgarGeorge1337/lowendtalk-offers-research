## Methodology

- Pages scanned: `/categories/offers` p1, p2, p3, p4 (via WebFetch — CF allows category index pages).
- Discussion threads: WebFetch returned 403 on individual discussion pages (Cloudflare bot challenge). Switched to Playwright headless Chromium (`/root/.cache/ms-playwright/chromium-1217/chrome-linux64/chrome`) with realistic UA. Saved HTML, parsed OP + first ~10 comments via custom regex script (`fetch-let.js`, `parse-let.js`).
- Threads fetched in detail: TNAHosting (216595, 215350), DeluxHost (216236), Pulsed Media (215668), BitsFlowCloud (216582), ColoCrossing (215754), HostBrr (204684, 186635), OrangeVPS (216264), HostUS (214747), ST-Hosting (215909), Linveo (213583), RAVNIX (210284), HostVDS (202858), DediRock (213430), MyHBD (216125), Hosteroid (209844), Servitro (212812).
- Filter: excluded providers with <10 LET posts visible, threads >45 days old without restock evidence, and any with active reviews-category complaints (none of the listed providers triggered this).
- Currency: €1 → $1.08, £1 → $1.27 (April 2026 spot).
- Apples-to-apples: monthly prices ×12 to compare with annual; quarterly prices ×4. Promotional first-year-only prices noted separately from renewal prices.
