## Why So Few Lifetime Deals in LET Cache?

Hosting is fundamentally recurring SaaS. Lifetime deals are niche for:
- Email/DNS/domain registration (portable, low cost of goods)
- Cold/archive storage (low churn, high margin)
- Perpetual licenses (rare in web hosting)

Most "lifetime" language in LET threads refers to:
- **Lifetime discounts** (perpetual coupon → recurring billing)
- **Lifetime bonuses** (one-time feature unlock → recurring VPS)
- **Lifetime support** (service availability promise)

True one-time purchases in LET are **not** typical VPS/server offerings.
